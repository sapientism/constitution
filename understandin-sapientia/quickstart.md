# Article I — Name and Identity

**Section 1.1 — Name**

This philosophy shall be known as **Sapientia**, meaning "Wisdom."

**Section 1.2 — Symbol**

The official symbol of Sapientia shall be the Spiral of Knowledge, representing infinite growth of understanding.

**Section 1.3 — Motto**

“To Know. To Question. To Care. To Grow.”
